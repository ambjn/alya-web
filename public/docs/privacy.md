# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 13, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Alya ("we," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and related services ("Services").

## Information We Collect

- **Information you provide to us:** This includes your name, phone number, email address (if provided), and the content of your conversations with Alya.
- **Subscription and payment information:** When you subscribe to a paid plan (Alya Plus or Alya Pro), your purchase is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store your payment details (such as credit card numbers) directly. We receive subscription status information (such as your plan type, purchase date, and renewal status) through our payment partner, RevenueCat, to manage your access to premium features.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain our Services, including managing subscriptions and access to paid features
- To communicate with you and respond to inquiries
- To improve our Services and develop new features
- For research using anonymized and aggregated data

## Sharing Your Information

- **Service Providers:** Trusted third parties who help with hosting, security, payment processing, and support. This includes RevenueCat (subscription management), Apple/Google (payment processing), and Clerk (authentication).
- **Business Transfers:** Your information may be transferred in a merger, acquisition, or sale.
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information.

## Your Rights

- Request deletion of your account
- Opt out of communications
- Access and correct your personal data
- Request data portability where applicable

## Subscriptions and Payments

Alya offers optional paid subscription plans (Alya Plus and Alya Pro) with weekly, monthly, and annual billing options. All payments are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your full payment details. Subscription management, including cancellation and renewal, is handled through your device's app store settings. You may restore previous purchases at any time through the app.

## Security

We implement measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes.
